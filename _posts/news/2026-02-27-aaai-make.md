We are thrilled to announce the acceptance of one paper for presentation at the upcoming [AAAI Spring Symposium on Machine Learning and Knowledge Engineering for Knowledge-Grounded Semantic Agents (MAKE 2026)](https://www.aaai-make.info), 
to be held in Burlingame (California, United States) from April 7 to April 9:
* "Failure Detection in Chemical Processes using Symbolic Machine Learning: A Case Study on Ethylene Oxidation" by Julien Amblard, Niklas Groll, Matthew Tait, Mark Law, Gürkan Sin and Alessandra Russo.
